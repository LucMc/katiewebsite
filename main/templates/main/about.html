About

A recent Zoology graduate (University of Leeds) working as an intern at London Array Offshore Windfarm. I have a strong interest in the environment and sustainability - working at London Array allows me to explore these interests further and do so in a way that helps me contribute towards fighting the climate crisis. Previous to working at London Array, I spent several months as an intern at Tesco, working in the technology department. This experience provided me with a strong techincal understanding and deepened my appreciated for the practical uses of technology. In the future, I would like to find a way to combine the skills I have learnt from both internships; perhaps by creating a new technology designed to positively impact the environment. In my free-time, I enjoy finding ways to combine sustainability with my other interests (such as business, economics and STEM subjects). For example, I manage a stocks and shares portfolio that focuses on sustainable development, run a small business that promotes the recycling of second-hand clothes and volunteer for Leeds City Museum on an environment based project that encourages young girls to pursue STEM subjects.
